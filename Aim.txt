Aim of our project 
 

• Allotting unique customer ID for each customer who books a room in hotel. 

• Updating the room list after allotting a room to a customer. 

• Generating unique book ID for each room booked.  

• Allotting a new vehicle for a customer (if desired) by referring booking ID. 

• Updating the vehicle list after allotting vehicle to a customer.
